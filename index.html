Cows and Bulls Instructions
  • Bull : If the digit in your number is present in answer and position is exactly matched
  • Cow : If the digit in your number is present in answer but the position is not exactly matched
  • Constarints
    • All the digits are unique & zero is not included
    • A digit considered for cow cant be considered for bull
  • Example :
    • Answer : 4271
    • Player's try: 1234
    • Result : 1 bull and 2 cows. (The bull is "2", the cows are "4" and "1".)
  • Player Wins if he gets n bulls for n-digit number
  • Progressbar indicates chances and life left
